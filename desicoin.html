Owner-Controlled Mint

Only the contract owner can mint new DESI tokens — strictly up to the 100M hard cap. Every new issuance is public and traceable on BscScan. No surprise inflation.

  uint256 amount)
  public onlyOwner {
  require(totalSupply() + amount
    <= MAX_SUPPLY,

How to Buy

Get DESI in 4 steps.

You need a BNB Smart Chain wallet and a small amount of BNB for gas — a few rupees worth is enough to start.

2

Swap BNB for DESI, confirm the transaction, and DESI lands in your wallet. That's it — you're in.
