About CTEA

A token built with care

ChaiTea (CTEA) is a community-oriented BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain, inspired by the warmth and simplicity of a good cup of chai.

The contract is open-source, verified, and deployed with a fixed maximum supply — ensuring full transparency for all holders.

  • Fixed max supply — Never more than 2 crore CTEA will ever exist
  • Verified source code — Publicly auditable on Sourcify
  • BNB Chain native — Fast transactions, low fees
  • Standard BEP-20 — Compatible with all major wallets and DEXes
